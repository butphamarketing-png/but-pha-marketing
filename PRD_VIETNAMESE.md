# PRD - AI SEO Content Platform

## 1. Tong quan

San pham la mot nen tang AI giup doi content va SEO:
- Tao bai viet tu title hoac keyword
- Danh gia chat luong SEO
- Theo doi rank keyword
- Phat hien bai tut hang
- Tao draft cap nhat tu dong
- Go y internal link va topical cluster
- Phan tich SERP va content opportunity
- Ho tro publish, index va dashboard

## 2. Van de can giai quyet

Team content thuong gap:
- Mat nhieu thoi gian viet outline va draft
- Kho danh gia bai co du chuan SEO hay khong
- Kho biet bai nao dang tut rank
- Kho cap nhat bai cu dung luc
- Kho xay topical authority co he thong

## 3. Muc tieu

- Rut ngan thoi gian tao bai
- Tang chat luong toi uu SEO
- Phat hien co hoi cap nhat som
- Tang kha nang mo rong he thong content
